Apple iPhone 13 in Starlight, 128 GB

Move over, white and silver. The newest iPhone 13 color, Starlight, pushes the boundaries of what Apple has done before in all directions and dimensions. This upgrade over the 12 moves faster, is built sturdier and has a longer battery life than any of its predecessors. And it's all engineered to weigh less than 6.5 ounces. That's thanks to its light but strong aluminum shell and Ceramic Shield front—some of the strongest smartphone glass designed yet. You'll need to worry less if it slips through your fingers (and even if it falls into the shower, bathtub or pool). The IP68 water resistance rating makes this shining Starlight device even tougher. It's splash, dust, and water-resistant up to a maximum depth of 6 meters for 30 minutes. Take beachside selfies as the sun sets, and take a closer look at the advanced dual-lens camera.

A Professional Photography Level Camera

The iPhone 13's wide and ultra-wide lenses allow users to capture nearly everything you can see, from expansive landscape shots to group photos. Take simple photos to save memories, or go in-depth with advanced control options. Adjust bokeh, play with six levels of Portrait Lighting, or engage Smart HDR 4 to help automatically correct elements like skin tone, contrast, lighting and more. Switch to portrait mode to blur the background of your space and focus on the subject for artfully crafted results.

Speaking of focus, move to video recording and switch to Cinematic mode to let the A15 bionic chip's Neural Engine take the reins. This machine learning miniature brain analyzes the scene and focuses naturally on the subject, then switches the focal point as the scene changes. Shoot within a shallow depth of field in HD (1080 p) too. Alternatively, record video at 4K and play it back later on a larger screen to see the most intimate, intricate details.

Got iPhone 13 Questions?

If you're hoping to learn more about Apple's latest mobile device, come to your local experts for help. Give our team a call at 800-860-3577 or head to our onsite Apple Store to learn more about these ultra-fast machines. Alternatively, do some research ahead of time with our September 2021 Apple Event.

Features

  • Flat-Edge Design
    • Features a flat-edge design with an aerospace-grade aluminum frame, available in five stunning finishes
    • Durable design features Ceramic Shield, industry-leading IP68 water resistance,1 and spill resistance to common liquids like coffee, tea, and soda
  • Battery
    • iPhone 13 will last more than 2.5 hours longer in your day than iPhone 12, and iPhone 13 mini will last more than 1.5 hours longer than iPhone 12 mini
  • Super Retina XDR Display
    • Brighter Super Retina XDR display has 800 nits peak brightness outdoors and delivers 1200 nits peak brightness for HDR photos and movies
    • Phone 13 mini has a 5.4-inch Super Retina XDR display and iPhone 13 has a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display
  • A15 Bionic with Neural Engine
    • A15 Bionic is the fastest chip ever in a smartphone, featuring a powerful new 6-core CPU with 2 high-performance and 4 high-efficiency cores, so demanding workloads run smoothly and efficiently
    • A15 Bionic features Apple’s latest 4-core GPU, so you’ll notice more lifelike visuals and lighting effects in graphicintensive tasks like advanced games
    • 16-core Neural Engine allows even faster machine learning computations, so you get experiences like Live Text in the Camera app
  • 12MP TrueDepth Camera with Face ID
    • The TrueDepth camera system, including Face ID, fits into a space that's 20 percent smaller for more display area
  • Advanced Dual-Camera System
    • Most advanced dual-camera system ever in an iPhone
    • All-new Wide camera has sensor-shift OIS, a !/1.6 aperture, and the largest sensor in a dual-camera iPhone for sharp images and great performance in low light
    • Custom-designed new Ultra Wide camera with a new sensor reveals more details in the dark areas of photos and videos with less noise
    • Cinematic mode automatically adds focus transitions that shift the focus or depth of field—and you can change the depth effect, including the level of bokeh and where the focus is applied, even after the video has been taken
    • Photographic Styles bring your individual preferences into Apple’s advanced image pipeline, applying tailored, local edits like a photo editor would, but in real time as the photo is rendered, while preserving skin tones
    • Smart HDR 4 automatically improves color, contrast, and lighting for multiple subjects in a photo
    • Take incredible low-light photos with Night mode on all cameras, including the TrueDepth camera
  • Connectivity
    • Apple is doubling 5G support to over 200 carriers, so you can benefit from 5G in more places than ever
    • With Dual SIM2 and eSIM, you can have two numbers in a single iPhone
  • MagSafe Accessories
    • Add Apple-designed MagSafe cases, charger, battery pack, leather wallet that you can see in the Find My app, and thirdparty MagSafe-compatible accessories
  • Essential Additions
    • Power adapter, AirPods, AirTag, AppleCare+ for iPhone, fast-charge accessories, cases, Apple Music, iCloud
